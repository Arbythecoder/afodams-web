## ‚ö†Ô∏è KNOWN LIMITATIONS (FREE Tier)

### Render.com:
- **Spin-down:** After 15min inactivity, backend sleeps
- **Wake-up time:** First request takes ~30 seconds
- **Solution:** Use UptimeRobot to ping every 14 minutes

### GitHub Pages:
- **No server-side routing:** That's why we use HashRouter
- **No backend:** Perfect for static frontend
- **Bandwidth:** 100GB/month (more than enough)

### MongoDB Atlas:
- **Storage:** 512MB FREE (enough for ~10,000 properties)
- **Connections:** Limited to 500 concurrent
- **Bandwidth:** More than sufficient for this use case
